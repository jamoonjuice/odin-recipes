Tacos

Description

Tacos are a popular Mexican dish consisting of a folded or rolled tortilla filled with a variety of ingredients. Common fillings include seasoned meats, beans, cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, and salsa. Tacos can be made with soft corn or flour tortillas, and they are often garnished with toppings like avocado, cilantro, and lime. Known for their vibrant flavors and versatility, tacos can be customized to suit any palate, making them a beloved choice for casual meals and festive gatherings alike.


Ingredients

  • Kidney beans
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 onion - chopped finely r
  • 6 garlic cloves - chopped finely
  • 1 cup tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoons oregano
  • 2 teaspoons cumin powder
  • T teaspoon pepper
  • 1 teaspoon red chilli flakes
  • 2 tablespoons pickled ginger
  • Taco shell
  • Cheese
  • Coriander leaves
  • Salt (as per requirement)

Steps

  1. Soak kidney beans overnight. Pressure cook for 4 to 5 whistle. Set aside.
  2. Heat oil and saute onions, garlic and chillies. Cook till golden.
  3. Add in beans along with cooking liquid, salt, all spices and mix well. Add in tomato puree and mix well.
  4. Mash it well using a masher.
  5. Simmer for 15 mins or so.
  6. Now bake taco shell in oven as per package directions.
  7. Fill with beans, cheese, onions, tomatoes, coriander and lettuce and serve.
